Interaction Design Comparative Research

Seize the Awkward

Seize the Awkward's website has some interactive elements that users can utilize to navigate through the campaign's services. There are drop down elements for navigation of different areas of the website, scroll-down elements that emphasize the messages provided by the campaign, and unique unique navigation. Although there are using ways interactivity is used to provide the messgage of destigmatizing conversations around mental health, there is not statistical data that users can interact with. I think this will be a much better website and campaign.

WoeBot

WoeBot is an app that allows users to track their mental health progress using CBT and DBT coping skills. The app tracks the users mood daily, how often they practice coping skills, and places them on a graph to show how the user's progress is. This is an exceptional application because there is some data being integrated and graphed for analytical evaluation.
